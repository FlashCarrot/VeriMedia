About VeriMedia

An AI-driven media analysis tool designed to enhance ethical reporting on refugee and migration issues.

Our Mission

VeriMedia is an innovative AI-driven media analysis tool developed for UNICC to enhance the capacity of media outlets to report ethically and accurately on topics related to refugees, migrants, and other forcibly displaced populations.

Our mission is to support the detection and prevention of xenophobic language, misinformation, and harmful content in media environments, ultimately fostering more informed and empathetic public discourse.

The Challenge

Media coverage plays a crucial role in shaping public perception and policy decisions regarding refugees and migrants. However, reporting on these topics can sometimes include harmful stereotypes, xenophobic language, or misinformation that contributes to negative attitudes and discrimination.

Journalists and media professionals often face challenges in identifying potentially harmful content in their reporting, especially when working under tight deadlines or when covering complex humanitarian situations.

Our Solution

VeriMedia leverages advanced artificial intelligence to analyze media content across multiple formats (text, audio, and video) and languages. The tool provides:

  • Toxicity Detection: Identification of potentially harmful or xenophobic language in content.
  • Improvement Suggestions: Actionable recommendations to enhance content inclusivity and accuracy.
  • Comprehensive Reports: Detailed analysis to understand areas for improvement.
  • Multi-language Support: Analysis capabilities across multiple languages for global reporting.
  • Multimodal Analysis: Support for text, audio, and video content analysis.
